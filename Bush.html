Vannevar Bush

Dr. Vannevar Bush describes in this article how much knowledge people have gathered over the years, and how useful means to store this knowledge would be. He claims it would be scientist’s role to make their knowledge more accessible to people.

As sir Francis Bacon said: “Knowledge is Power”. But everyone should have access to this knowledge. Otherwise, scientist’s role would be useless. And it is quite fascinating to see how Dr. Vannevar Bush predicted what would become later our future devices which would allow us to store information.
