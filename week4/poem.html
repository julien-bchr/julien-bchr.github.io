SOLMAZ SHARIF
Abstract
Personal Effects by Paisley Rekdal is a poem that explores the theme of grief and loss. The speaker of the poem is sorting through the personal effects of a loved one who has passed away. As the speaker examines each item, they reflect on memories associated with that object, leading to a meditation on the nature of grief, memory, and the fragility of human life.
